Advanced XML: Time-based charting

This view is similar to the advanced charting view. However, it contains a timeline that is fully functional to narrow down, zoom in, and zoom out.

The JSChart module implements a drilldown pattern – you can click elements to see the corresponding events below.

Type in any reporting search, for example:

index=_internal source=*metrics.log group=per_sourcetype_thruput
| chart avg(eps) by series | sort 10 - avg(eps)

Pre-populate this view with saved searches

You can pre-populate this view from the Nav Menu with saved searches, which are defined in savedsearches.conf. Go to the following menu items to pre-populate the view:

Advanced XML > Exploring reports in time > Custom charting by sourcetype
Advanced XML > Exploring reports in time > Custom charting by source

The Nav menu defines the menu items that specify the saved searches: 

. . .
<collection label="Exploring reports in time">
  <view name="custom_charting1"/>
  <saved name="Custom charting by sourcetype" />
  <saved name="Custom charting by source" />
</collection>
. . .

Here are the locations for the source of the Nav Menu and savedsearches.conf.

$SPLUNK_HOME/etc/apps/dashboard_examples/default/data/ui/nav/default.xml
$SPLUNK_HOME/etc/apps/dashboard_examples/default/savedsearches.conf
